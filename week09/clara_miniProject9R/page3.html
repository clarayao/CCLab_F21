Impression of the poem in the movie Interstellar

This poem is constantly brought up in the famous movie Interstellar.

  • The purpose of this is to indicate the main characters' struggle to fight for the survival of mankind, which is paralleled to the central theme of this film.

  • It also serves as a clue in the film to guide the audiences through all the major struggles in the film.

  • At the same time, the deep thinking about death and the depth of this poem, adds texture to the movie, evoking audiences to think more profoundly.
